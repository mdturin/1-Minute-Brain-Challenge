1 Minute Brain Challenge — Data Deletion Request

✉️ How to Request Deletion

Send an email to the address below with the subject line "Delete my account". Include the email address associated with your account so we can locate it.

✉  oneminutebrain@gmail.com
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you want to permanently remove all your data before submitting a request.

🗑️ What Gets Deleted

The following data will be permanently removed within 30 days of your request:

  • Your account credentials (email address and password hash)
  • Game scores, best scores, and average scores
  • Streak history and puzzle stats
  • Daily challenge completion records
  • All Firestore documents linked to your user ID

📋 What Is Not Deleted

The following information is not linked to your account and is not affected:

  • Anonymous or aggregated leaderboard statistics (not tied to your identity)
  • Local device data stored on your phone (clear this manually in your device settings)

⏱️ What Happens Next

1
We receive your email We'll send a confirmation reply within 2 business days.
2
Account is deactivated Your account is disabled immediately upon verification.
3
Data is permanently deleted All associated data is erased within 30 days of your request.
